General
Ballmecca is a sports coaching marketplace that connects athletes with verified coaches through short video sessions. Athletes upload a clip of their performance, a coach reviews it and sends back 1–5 minutes of personalized feedback and drills. It's elite coaching made affordable and accessible — anytime, anywhere.
We currently cover basketball, baseball, softball, soccer, football, tennis, golf, swimming, track & field, gymnastics, hockey, lacrosse, wrestling, rowing, and running. Dance is coming soon — download the app to be notified when it launches.
Download the Ballmecca app from the App Store or Google Play. Create an account, choose whether you're an athlete or a coach, and you'll be guided through the rest. It takes less than 5 minutes.
For Athletes
Browse coaches in your sport, select one, and purchase a session. Record a short video of your technique — a swing, a throw, a serve, a run. Upload it directly to your coach. They'll send back a personalized video analysis (1–5 minutes) with specific feedback and drills tailored to you.
Most coaches respond within 24–48 hours. Response time is displayed on each coach's profile. If a coach hasn't responded within the stated time, you can contact us at support@ballmecca.com.

For Coaches
Download the app, create a coach account, and complete your profile. Our team will review your application and verify your credentials. Once approved, you can set your rate, choose your sports, and start accepting sessions.
Payments are processed securely through Stripe with payouts to your bank account. You set your own rate per session. Ballmecca takes a small platform fee; the majority goes directly to you.
Coaches earn profit sharing whenever an athlete they refer purchases a coaching session — receiving 50% of Ballmecca's fee on those sessions. It's our way of rewarding coaches who grow the community.
